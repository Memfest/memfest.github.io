Celebrating the Life of Memorie King

Thank you for visiting our website in honor of Memorie King. Memorie was a beautiful soul taken too early from the world. This website is for all those who have been blessed with the privilage of having known Memorie.

This gesamtkunstwerk is devoted to honor Memorie King and includes:

  • Memfest I (Sept 2015 - to be continued)
  • Postcards available at two for $1 plus postage, call the number below
  • The herb barn shrine
  • A bronze memorial
  • A kirtan (in planning stage)

We will continue to honor and remember her spirit through music, photos, and stories.

Most recently Memorie's husband Loch Hoffman, has started a memorial scholarship at the North Carolina Herb Association. For more information about the Memorie King Scholarship, please click here.

Please feel free to explore our website for stories and photos of Memorie. If you would like to share any photos or stories about Memorie please email Loch.
